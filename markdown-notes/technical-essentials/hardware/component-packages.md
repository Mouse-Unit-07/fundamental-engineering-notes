## Passive SMT

- 1206, 0805, 0603, 0402, 0201, 01005
  - Metric chip resistor, capacitor, etc
- 1210, 1812
  - Power resistor / high-voltage capacitor
- MLCC
  - "multi-layer ceramic capacitor"

## Special/Misc

- MELF
  - "metal electrode leadless face"
  - A cylindrical resistor/diode SMT
- PGA
  - "pin grid array"
  - CPU socket ICs
- FCBGA
  - "flip-chip ball grid array"
  - BGA variant w/ flip-chip die
- LCC
  - "leadless chip carrier"
  - Old IC package, rectangular, leadless sides
- QFN/DFN
  - "leadless ICs"
  - Small and good for thermal
- PLCC
  - "plastic leadless chip carrier"
  - Square w/ J-leads- legacy ICs now
- COB
  - "chip-on-board"
  - A bare die mounted on a PCB- encapsulated functionality

## Surface-Mount

- SOT-23
  - "small outline transistor"
  - 3-6 pins, small discrete transistors or ICs
- SOT-223
  - Large power transistors, voltage regulators
- SOT-89
  - 3-pin, slightly bigger than SOT-23
- SOIC/SO
  - "small outline IC"
- TSSOP
  - "thin shrink small outline package"
  - Common for flash memory/ROM
- SSOP
  - "shrink small outline package"
- QFP
  - "quad flat package"
- TQFP
  - "thin quad flat package"
- LQFP
  - "low-profile QFP"
- QFN
  - "quad flat no-lead"
- MLF
  - "micro lead frame"
- DFN
  - "dual flat no-lead"
- BGA
  - "ball grid array"
  - Balls on the bottom of the IC for dense/high pin count
- CSP/WLCSP
  - "chip scale package" / "wafer-level CSP"
  - Very small, direct solder balls, and minimal footprint
- VSOP
  - "very small outline package"
  - Small QFP variant
- SOP
  - "small outline package"
  - ...a legacy name
- MSOP
  - "mini small outline package"

## Through-Hole

- DIP/PDIP
  - "dual in-line package", "plastic DIP"
  - Old/classic IC package
- SIP
  - "single in-line package"
  - Usually for resistors, regulators, LEDs
- TO-92
  - "transistor outline"
  - 3-pin, small signal transistors, temp sensors, etc
- TO-220
  - Power semiconductors, 3+ pins, have heat sink tabs
- TO-247
  - Higher power semiconductors
- TO-3
  - Metal can package- classic power transistors
- TO-263
  - D2PAK, etc
  - Medium power
- Axial/Radial
  - Resistors, capacitors, diodes
