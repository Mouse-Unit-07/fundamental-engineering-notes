## Overview

- Zigbee is another wireless interface similar to Bluetooth
- Zigbee’s “mesh networking” allows for large, connected networks in contrast to Bluetooth
  - Devices can communicate w/ each other by relaying messages between devices
  - Has low data rate of 250 kbps
- Disadvantages include:
  - Low distance between devices themselves (10-100m)
  - Low data rate as mentioned
